Our mission

At Sheffield Hallam University, our valued staff members, teachers, professors and members have pledged to maintain and protect all that we care about; you, the students. Sheffield Hallam University's mission is to ensure you receive a strong and solid education for your future careers, and acquire all the skills and abilities you need to succeed.
